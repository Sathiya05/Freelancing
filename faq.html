General Questions

What makes you different from cheap resume mills?
Resume mills use automated templates and outsource writing overseas to people who have never worked in corporate HR. I personally work 1-on-1 with you. You are paying for my 10+ years of insider recruiting experience, strategic positioning, and understanding of recruiter psychology, not just a typing service.
Do you guarantee I will get a job?
No ethical coach can guarantee a job, as final hiring decisions are entirely out of our control. However, I *do* guarantee a significant increase in your interview request rate. If you don't see a 2x increase in recruiter callbacks within 60 days of using your new assets, I will rewrite your documents for free.
I'm pivoting to a totally new industry. Can you still help?
Absolutely. Career pivots are one of my specialties. We will focus entirely on your *transferable skills*—translating the impact you made in your past career into the language and metrics that hiring managers in your new target industry care about.

The Process

How long does the whole process take?
Standard turnaround is 5-7 business days after our initial 60-minute discovery call. The total timeline depends on how quickly you provide feedback during the revision stage. On average, clients have their final, market-ready documents in their hands within 10 days.
What formats will I receive my resume in?
You will receive a perfectly formatted, ATS-compliant PDF document for your immediate applications, as well as an fully editable Microsoft Word (.DOCX) file so you can make minor tweaks and updates to your resume in the future without needing to hire me again.
How many revisions do I get?
All packages include two (2) rounds of comprehensive revisions. I want to ensure your documents sound authentically like *you* while maintaining the strategic framing necessary to get you hired.

Pricing & Payment

Do you offer payment plans?
Yes! I understand that investing in your career is a big step. We partner with Klarna and Afterpay at checkout, allowing you to split your payment into 4 interest-free installments.
Can I upgrade my package later?
Absolutely. If you start with just the Resume Rewrite and decide a week later that you also want LinkedIn Optimization or Mock Interview Prep, I will honor the discounted "Bundle" pricing and simply invoice you for the difference.
